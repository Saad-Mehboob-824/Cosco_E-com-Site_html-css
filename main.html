We have made an online shopping store for the brand name of “Cosco”. Keeping it user friendly and professional we chose a white background with animated figures (People and items) on it. The starting page displays an animated figure of a man holding groceries and right of the figure we ask the user for his Username and Password. The home page will also display a large and blue colored “Cosco” logo. Beneath all this, are two buttons, one for registration and one for login. Both the buttons have a “blue” touch to them to match nicely with the Cosco logo. Upon pressing the “Register” button, the user will be directed to the registration page that also displays a large animated figure holding groceries but rather than the Cosco logo displays a welcome text. Over here the user will enter the required information (his first name, last name, age, email address, username and his password). Beneath these boxes where the user will enter his information is another blue colored button by the name of “Submit”. Upon pressing the “Submit” button from the registration page or pressing the “login” Button from the starting page, the user will be guided to the homepage of the web store that will show a large, blue “Cosco” logo at the top. The theme has again been kept consistent with a black-white theme. Our background here shall be white and there will be a search bar on the extreme left hand side while the option for cart and user account will be on the right hand side. There is a picture of groceries on the bottom right side of the page while the different categories of items are displayed using black icons on the bottom left hand side. Beneath the categories are the top seller items, where the specific item its own in a separate white themed box. Each individual box consists of the item name in black, its colored picture and the price of the item. There are several such items listed in such manner as the user scrolls down. Upon pressing any category the user is shown a “Sub-page” where only items of the specific and selected category are shown. The theme has again been kept the same with white themed boxes having an item’s name, its picture and his price at the bottom.
